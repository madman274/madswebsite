Maddy Weavers is an editor and consultant for art and architecture publications and organisations. She is deputy editor of [Real Review](https://real-review.org), previously holding editorial roles at [Apartamento](https://www.apartamentomagazine.com/archive/madelon-vriesendorp/) and [Drawing Matter](https://drawingmatter.org). She is currently working with [Guggenheim Museum Bilbao](https://www.guggenheim-bilbao.eus/en/the-collection) as a consultant for [Jane Wentworth Associates](https://janewentworth.com). She has collaborated with [Bofill Taller de Arquitectura](https://www.bofill.com/journal/whats-old-is-new-again/) for a number of years, editing the first monograph dedicated to a single building by the studio, which will be published in 2026. She trained in architecture and worked in practice before moving into editorial work. Her writing has featured in publications including [AA Files](https://www.aaschool.ac.uk/public/publications), [MacGuffin](https://www.macguffinmagazine.com/issues/macguffin-the-wall), and [The Architectural Review](https://www.architectural-review.com). 
For commissions or enquiries please contact maddylweavers@gmail.com.
