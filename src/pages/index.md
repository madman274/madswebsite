Maddy Weavers is an editor and strategic consultant specialising in art, design and architecture publications and organisations. She trained in architecture, and her writing on the subject has featured in publications including [AA Files](https://www.aaschool.ac.uk/public/publications), [MacGuffin](https://www.macguffinmagazine.com/issues/macguffin-the-wall), and [The Architectural Review](https://www.architectural-review.com). She is deputy editor of [Real Review](https://real-review.org); she previously held editorial roles at [Apartamento](https://www.apartamentomagazine.com/archive/madelon-vriesendorp/) and [Drawing Matter](https://drawingmatter.org). Maddy is currently working with [Guggenheim Museum Bilbao](https://www.guggenheim-bilbao.eus/en/the-collection) as a consultant for [Jane Wentworth Associates](https://janewentworth.com). She regularly collaborates with [Bofill Taller de Arquitectura](https://www.bofill.com/journal/whats-old-is-new-again/); she is editing the first monograph dedicated to a single building by Bofill, which will be published in 2026.
For commissions or enquiries please contact maddylweavers@gmail.com.
