Maddy Weavers is an editor and consultant for art, design and architecture publications and organisations. She is deputy editor of [Real Review](https://real-review.org), having previously held roles at [Apartamento](https://www.apartamentomagazine.com/archive/madelon-vriesendorp/) and [Drawing Matter](https://drawingmatter.org). She is currently collaborating with [Guggenheim Museum Bilbao](https://www.guggenheim-bilbao.eus/en/the-collection) as a consultant for [Jane Wentworth Associates](https://janewentworth.com). She regularly collaborates with [Bofill Taller de Arquitectura](https://www.bofill.com/journal/whats-old-is-new-again/); she is now working on the first monograph dedicated to a single building by Bofill, which will be published in 2026. She trained in architecture and has written around the subject for publications including [AA Files](https://www.aaschool.ac.uk/public/publications), [MacGuffin](https://www.macguffinmagazine.com/issues/macguffin-the-wall), and [The Architectural Review](https://www.architectural-review.com). For commissions or enquiries please contact maddylweavers@gmail.com.
